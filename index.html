FAQ

1. 行程可以臨時調整嗎？

客製化行程最大優點就是彈性。出發前三至七天如有狀況，都可以與我們討論調整，僅需配合當地空位情形。

2. 什麼時候去富國島天氣最好？

11 月到隔年 4 月為乾季，海水顏色最漂亮、浪比較小，是多數旅人首選月份。

3. 要怎麼換越南盾比較划算？

可以先帶美金或台幣，到機場或當地金店兌換。部分車資與門票也可接受刷卡或電子支付。

4. 富國島治安如何？適合帶長輩小孩嗎？

富國島是越南治安相對友善的區域，步調悠閒，樂園與多數公共空間皆有無障礙設施，很適合三代同遊。

富國島 10 項常見問題10 common Phu Quoc questions

1. 行程可以臨時調整嗎？1. Can the itinerary be adjusted at the last minute?

客製化行程的優勢在於彈性，送出預約表單後，如遇狀況，請於出發日前三至七天與我們溝通調整。建議送出前先確認初步規劃。Custom trips are flexible. After you submit the booking form, please contact us 3 to 7 days before departure if changes are needed. We recommend confirming the draft plan before submitting.

2. 什麼時候去富國島天氣最好？2. When is the best weather in Phu Quoc?

11 月至 4 月是乾季，天氣穩定且海水最透，是最佳旅遊時機。November to April is the dry season, when the weather is stable and the sea is clearest. It is the best travel period.

3. 要怎麼換越南盾比較划算？3. What is the best way to exchange Vietnamese dong?

可攜帶美金或台幣到機場、當地金店兌換。Grab 叫車可綁台灣信用卡，非常便利。You can bring USD or TWD to exchange at the airport or local gold shops. Grab can also be linked with a Taiwan credit card, which is very convenient.

4. 富國島治安如何？適合帶長輩小孩嗎？4. How safe is Phu Quoc? Is it suitable for seniors and children?

治安極佳且島民友善，是越南最安全的地區之一。適合親子與長輩同行。Safety is excellent and locals are friendly, making it one of the safest areas in Viet Nam. It is suitable for families and seniors.

5. 要給小費嗎？5. Should I tip?

非強迫性，但建議飯店搬運或床頭可給 20,000~50,000 越南盾表達謝意。Tipping is optional, but 20,000 to 50,000 VND is a nice gesture for hotel porters or housekeeping.

6. 什麼電壓？插頭能不能直接用？6. What is the voltage? Can I use my plugs directly?

220V 電壓，插座為兩孔圓形或扁形通用，一般台灣充電器可直接使用。The voltage is 220V, and sockets are usually compatible with two-pin round or flat plugs. Most Taiwan chargers can be used directly.

7. 直飛富國島需要簽證嗎？7. Do I need a visa for direct flights to Phu Quoc?

直飛富國島免簽證 30 天。若經由河內或胡志明轉機則需電子簽 (E-Visa)。Direct flights to Phu Quoc are usually visa-free for 30 days. If you transfer via Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City, an e-visa is usually required.

8. 富國島有什麼親子景點？8. What family-friendly attractions are in Phu Quoc?

適合！有無障礙設施良好的樂園與動物園，是親子首選。Yes. Theme parks and zoos with good accessibility make Phu Quoc a top family choice.

9. 網路要怎麼處理？9. How should I handle mobile data or internet?

建議自行購買網路卡或機場抵達後現場購買也非常方便。We recommend buying a SIM card yourself, or purchasing one at the airport upon arrival, which is also very convenient.

10. 遇到緊急狀況怎麼辦？10. What if I have an emergency?

如遇緊急狀況，凡預約客製服務，我們提供 24 小時中文緊急聯絡電話。If an emergency occurs, customers who book custom services receive a 24/7 Mandarin emergency contact number.
